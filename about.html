Our vision

Reverie AI is designed to help users reconnect with their loved ones through simulated conversations. This isn't just a voicebot— it’s a way to reconnect, process emotions, and find comfort through the voices of those we miss the most.

Our goal is to make grief support more accessible by leveraging AI to provide companionship and emotional relief. We strive to create a safe, compassionate, and always-available space where users can engage in meaningful interactions with the voices of their loved ones.

Our Research

This is what we found through our survey data.

Demographic
The data highlights that many young adults (ages 18-25) have experienced loss (Figure 1), but are hesitant about therapy, citing reasons such as privacy concerns, lack of human empathy in AI, and a preference for human interaction.
Interest
From Figure 2, we can see that around 42.8% said they might be interested in using an AI chatbot for grief support.
User Feedback
Of this group, 48.6% of participants were interested in an app that contained features of Daily check-ins & mood-tracking, reflection and journal prompts (Figure 3 & 4).
